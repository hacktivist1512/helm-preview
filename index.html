How it works

Three steps from
thought to done

Step 01
You type
Describe the task naturally. Helm parses intent, identifies the right action, and pulls context from your current session.
Step 02
Helm plans
A confirmation card shows you exactly what will happen — parameters, affected records, reversibility — before a single API call is made.
Step 03
You confirm
One click. Helm executes against the platform's native APIs at full speed, then reports back with the result and any follow-up options.
